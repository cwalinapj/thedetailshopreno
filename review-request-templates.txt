TIPS FOR GETTING MORE REVIEWS
================================================================================

1. ASK RIGHT AFTER SERVICE
   - Best time: when customer sees their clean car and is happy
   - Say: "If you're happy, a Google review would really help us out!"

2. MAKE IT EASY
   - Send the direct link (not "search for us on Google")
   - Text is better than email (higher response rate)

3. DON'T OFFER INCENTIVES
   - Google prohibits discounts/gifts for reviews
   - Can get your reviews removed

4. RESPOND TO ALL REVIEWS
   - Thank positive reviewers by name
   - Address negative reviews professionally

5. TIMING
   - Send request within 24 hours of service
   - Follow up once after 3-5 days, then stop

6. GOAL
   - Aim for 20+ reviews in first 2 months
   - Then 2-4 new reviews per month ongoing
